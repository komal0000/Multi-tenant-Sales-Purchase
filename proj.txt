📘 PROJECT DOCUMENT
Sales, Purchase & Ledger System (Laravel + MySQL)
🎯 OBJECTIVE

Build a single-business accounting system with:

Parties (no customer/supplier separation)
Sales bills (manual item entry)
Purchase bills (manual item entry)
Payments (linked or advance)
Cash & Bank accounts
Ledger-based balance system
🧠 CORE PRINCIPLE (MOST IMPORTANT)

✅ Ledger is the only source of truth
❌ Never store or manually update balance
✅ All balances must be calculated from ledger

🧱 MODULES
Parties
Sales
Purchases
Payments
Accounts (Cash/Bank)
Ledger

1. Parties

Purpose: Store all people/entities

Fields:

id (BIGINT AUTO INCREMENT)

Purpose: Store money locations

🔥 8. Ledger (CORE TABLE)

Fields:

id (BIGINT AUTO INCREMENT)
party_id (nullable)
account_id (nullable)

👉 Only ONE must be filled

dr_amount
cr_amount

👉 Only one should be > 0

type (sale / purchase / payment)
ref_id
ref_table
created_at
⚠️ LEDGER RULES (STRICT)
Each ledger row affects only ONE entity:
Either party OR account
Never fill both party_id and account_id
Never leave both empty
Only one of:
dr_amount OR cr_amount must be filled
Ledger entries must NEVER be deleted
Every transaction must create ledger entries
📊 ACCOUNTING RULES (DR / CR)
For Party
Situation	DR	CR
Party owes you	✔
You owe party		✔
Payment received		✔
Payment given	✔
For Cash / Bank
Situation	DR	CR
Money comes in	✔
Money goes out		✔
🧾 TRANSACTION FLOWS
1. SALE

Meaning:

Party buys from you → they owe you

Ledger:

party_id filled
DR = total
CR = 0
2. PURCHASE

Meaning:

You buy from party → you owe them

Ledger:

party_id filled
DR = 0
CR = total
3. PAYMENT RECEIVED

Meaning:

Party pays you

Create 2 ledger entries:

Party:
CR = amount
Account:
DR = amount
4. PAYMENT GIVEN

Meaning:

You pay party

Create 2 entries:

Party:
DR = amount
Account:
CR = amount
🔗 PAYMENT LINKING RULES
If payment is for a sale → set sale_id
If for purchase → set purchase_id
If advance → both NULL
📦 BILL RULES
Sales & Purchases
Must contain at least 1 item
Each item:
qty × price = total
Bill total:
sum of all item totals
📊 BALANCE CALCULATION
Party Balance
Balance = SUM(DR) - SUM(CR)

Meaning:

Positive → party owes you
Negative → you owe party
Account Balance
Balance = SUM(DR) - SUM(CR)
🔄 DELETE & UPDATE RULES
Sales / Purchases
Use soft delete (deleted_at)
DO NOT delete ledger
Payments
Must reverse ledger entries

Options:

Delete related ledger rows
OR create reverse entries (recommended)

⚠️ STRICT DEVELOPMENT RULES

❌ Do NOT store balance column
❌ Do NOT update balance manually
❌ Do NOT skip ledger
